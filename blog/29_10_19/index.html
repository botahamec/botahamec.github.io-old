I'm Too Busy

Hello, this is just a reminder that you need to balance your time. I can't do that. There's so much I want to do and not nearly enough for me to do it. I wish there was more time in this world. Too much is consumed by sleep and work, and not enough is spared for what you love. I want to work on my music, my AI, my tools, my hobbies, my entertainment, my education. But how much of it can I really do? I don't think I'll ever get everything done. I could dedicate my life to these things, but then how could I support myself? Working enough to support these things means that I no longer have time to do them.

I don't want to have money in my life. I want to do what I love, be with the people I love, and not worry about how to pay for my housing. Right now, I'm at the greatest point in my life. I don't have work, other than education. I still have time to work on my projects (but still not enough). I am, however, still removed from my loved ones. I feel it is too large of a price.

Maybe there is a career that will allow me to have free time. Honestly, I'm pretty sure as a programmer I would. But how could I ever justify coding for work for eight hours a day, just to go and code for another two hours. It's too much of the same thing. I need variety in my life. I considered double majoring in Math Education, but does a math teacher really have that much free time between grading assignments? What is there? I doubt I could get a job in music composition. I was denied entry into such a major. I imagine even majoring in Music Composition means majoring in unemployment.

Maybe I can wait until I become popular enough with my independent work, but do I really have the time? Ah, time! My greatest foe. I have maybe 65 years left of life, which will appear to go by much quicker as they progress. *Sobs*
